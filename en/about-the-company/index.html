About us
Clover Group is a Russian developer of solutions in the field of predictive maintenance services for the railway, energy, engineering, and oil and gas industries.

Clover Group solutions transform typical approaches to technical maintenance and repair (MRO) using AI technologies.

The company cooperates with the largest enterprises in Russia, the CIS, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia in the application of predictive analytics, as well as with leading scientific and educational institutions specializing in research in the fields of power engineering industry, oil and gas, metalworking, and exact sciences.

Our mission
We help industrial enterprises transform themselves through intellectual analytics. We are laying the foundation for the development of the ecosystem of the industrial Internet, making artificial intelligence technologies available for production companies.
